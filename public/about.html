About Paper Citation

What is Paper Citation?

Paper Citation is an AI-powered tool that helps you find academic sources for any claim and instantly generate properly formatted citations. Whether you're writing a research paper, blog post, or social media content, we make it easy to back up your statements with credible academic research.

Who is it for?

Paper Citation is designed for students, researchers, journalists, content creators, and anyone who needs to find credible sources quickly. Our tool is especially useful for:

  • Students writing essays and research papers
  • Journalists fact-checking claims
  • Content creators adding credibility to their work
  • Researchers exploring new topics
  • Academic professionals needing quick citation formatting

How it works

Paper Citation combines cutting-edge AI technology with comprehensive academic databases:

  1. AI Analysis: Our system analyzes your claim and extracts relevant academic search terms using advanced AI
  2. Database Search: We search OpenAlex, the world's largest academic database with over 250 million scholarly works
  3. Citation Generation: Papers are automatically formatted in APA, MLA, Chicago, and Harvard citation styles

Powered by OpenAlex + AI

Paper Citation leverages OpenAlex, the comprehensive open academic database that indexes research papers, authors, and institutions worldwide. Combined with sophisticated AI analysis, we can match your claims to the most relevant peer-reviewed research and generate accurate citations instantly.

Why we built this

Finding credible academic sources and formatting citations shouldn't be difficult. We created Paper Citation to remove the friction between having an idea and finding the evidence to support it. Our mission is to make academic research accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise or institutional access.
