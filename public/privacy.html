Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

Our Privacy Commitment

Paper Citation is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe you shouldn't have to trade your privacy for access to useful tools. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

No Account Required

Paper Citation does not require any account registration or personal information to use our service. You can find academic sources and generate citations immediately without creating an account, providing an email address, or any personal details.

No Personal Data Stored

We do not store, collect, or retain any personal information. When you use Paper Citation, your searches are processed anonymously. We don't create profiles, track users across sessions, or maintain databases of user information.

Search Privacy

Your searches are not logged or associated with your identity. We do not record your search history, claims you submit, or papers you discover. Each session is isolated and temporary.

Cloudflare-Powered Infrastructure

Paper Citation runs on Cloudflare's infrastructure, which provides enhanced privacy and security. Cloudflare's privacy-first approach ensures your requests are handled with appropriate security measures and privacy protections.

Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with OpenAlex, an open academic database, and uses Google's Gemini AI for search term generation. These services process your requests as described in our service, but we do not pass any personal information to them.

Cookies and Local Storage

Paper Citation does not use cookies or local storage to track your activity. Any temporary data stored in your browser during a session is limited to the current page load and is cleared when you close the tab.

Website Analytics

We do not use website analytics, tracking pixels, or other monitoring tools that would track your usage patterns or collect information about your browsing behavior.

Data Security

While we don't store personal data, we implement appropriate security measures to protect the integrity and availability of our service. All communications are secured with HTTPS encryption.

Children's Privacy

Paper Citation does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, our service is inherently compliant with children's privacy regulations.

International Users

Paper Citation is designed to be privacy-compliant worldwide. Since we don't collect personal data, our service aligns with international privacy standards including GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to our privacy practices, we will update this page with the new date. Given our privacy-first approach, significant changes are unlikely, but we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at info@papercitation.com.
